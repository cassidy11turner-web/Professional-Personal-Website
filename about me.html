Hello! I am Cassidy Halcomb! I have been teaching middle grade Language Arts for the past four years! I graduated with my Bachelor's Degree from EKU and am currently working on my Master's in school librarian.
I am orginally from Breathitt county, this is where I was born and raised, however, after student teaching at Clark Moore's Middle School in Richmond KY, I knew that is where I wanted to be and have been lucky enough to work there!

I have a two year old daughter, Olive Leona, with my husband, Matthew, and we have two cats, Twix and Phoenix.I have one brother and five sisters and I am the third oldest!

I have always loved education and my goal has always been to instill a love of reading within my students and to share my love of literature with them!

When I am not teaching, I am watching Frozen with Ollie, gardening, or reading a good book!
