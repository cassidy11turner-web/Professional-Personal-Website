Code of Ethics

Personal Code of Ethics

I will act with honesty. I will not misrepresent facts, take credit for work I did not do, or make promises I do not intend to keep. When I make a mistake, I will acknolwedge it, correct it, and learn from it.

Professional Code of Ethics

I will conduct myself with professionalism, accountability, and respect. I will meet my commitments, communicate clearly, protect confidential nformation, and avoid actions that could harm those I represent and teach.

Teaching Code of Conduct

I will create a fair, respectful, and supportive learning environment. I will treat students consistently, protect their privacy, encourage honest effort, and avoid bias. I will model the behavior I expect from students and continue improving my own knowledge and teaching practice.
