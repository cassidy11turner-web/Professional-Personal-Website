Running Past Dark Han Nolan Mystery/Thriller 14 & up 3 out of 5 stars Nolan does a wonderful job at exploring the complexities of grief and how life continues even in the face of tragedy
The Infinity Particle Wendy Xu Sci-Fi Graphic Novel 14 & up 1 out of 5 stars The artwork in this novel was beautiful, however, I felt like the character and plot developing was lacking. I could not get invested in the characters or the story itself. Overall, I found the concept and the book itself a bit lackluster.
One Step Forward Marcie Flinchum Atkins Historical Fiction 13 & up 5 out of 5 stars When I started this books, I was not certain that I would enjoy it because I do not really read a lot of historical fiction or novels in verse. However, the story of Matilda Young sucked me in from the beginning and I felt so many emotions throughout this book. Overall, it was a wonderful read and I am so glad I picked it up!
All the Fighting Parts Hannah V. Sawyerr YA Novel in verse 14 & Up 4 out of 5 stars This book was a powerful walkthrough of a teenager struggling throughout a sexual assault of a respected pastor in the community and her progression through dealing with this and fighting for justice for herself. Overall, a powerful and impactful novel.
Now She's Dead Roselyn Clark Mystery Thriller 14 & up 3 out of 5 stars While this was not a bad book and was by no means poorly written, I felt like it began to feel repetative and eventually I was able to anticipate the ending. Overall, a good option in the YA space.
