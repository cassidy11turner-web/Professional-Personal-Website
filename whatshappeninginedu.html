House Bill 257

What is this?

House Bill 257 was created as a new section of KRS Chapter 154 in order to define terms and permit school districts to deveoop and implement a local accountability program, which is reall just a fancy way of saying that it got rid of some sections of the KSA and that school districts can decide how some specific standards will measures and assessed.

What does this mean for Madison County?

This bill basically means that next year 5th & 8th graders will not have to complete the KSA On Demand Test. However, that does not mean that they will not have any type of writing or be required to complete writing. This just means that it will be up to the specific school district what this will look like, whether it is students completing writing portfolios or other types of writing.
